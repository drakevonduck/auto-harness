# Canonical And Derivative Records

Canonical records are the durable source of truth. Derivative records summarize, narrate, or aid local workflow.

## Canonical

- ADRs
- product requirements and release intent artifacts
- project delivery logs and milestone records
- program coordination artifacts when active
- risk register
- incident records
- migration records
- release records

## Derivative

- session logs
- scratchpads
- dev journals
- transient planning notes
- convenience summaries produced by agents

Derivative records may be useful. They are not authoritative.
